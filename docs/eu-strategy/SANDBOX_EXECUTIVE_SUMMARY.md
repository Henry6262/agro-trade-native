## What We Do

Project Grain Sovereign is a **digital public infrastructure** initiative that enables cross-border agrifood trade across the EU by replacing paper-based quality certificates with **cryptographically verifiable digital credentials** on the European Blockchain Services Infrastructure (EBSI).

Our platform (AgroTrade) already connects farmers, buyers, transporters, and inspectors via mobile-first stablecoin escrow. We are now extending this into the EU institutional stack:
- **EBSI Trusted Issuer** accreditation for freelance agrifood inspectors
- **Data Act B2G API** for real-time food-security monitoring
- **AI Act sandbox testing** for voice-AI tools assisting non-literate farmers

## Regulatory Frontier

We operate at the intersection of **three converging EU regulations**:

1. **EU Data Act (2023/2854)** — Mandatory B2G data sharing under "exceptional need" (Articles 14–22)
2. **EBSI / eIDAS 2.0** — Cross-border verifiable credentials for quality certification
3. **EU AI Act (2024/1689)** — High-risk classification of voice-AI for essential service access (Annex III)

No precedent exists for private agrifood inspectors as EBSI Trusted Issuers. No agrifood-specific AI Act sandbox has validated farmer-facing voice assistants. We generate the **regulatory evidence** that both industry and authorities need.

## Sandbox Trial Design

| Component | Activity | Output |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **EBSI VC Issuance** | 5 inspectors issue 50 grain-quality VCs on EBSI testnet | Validated W3C VC schema |
| **Cross-Border Verification** | German buyers verify VCs via TIR without contacting issuer | <500 ms verification time |
| **AI Oversight** | 20 farmers use voice-AI under human oversight | Bias audit + safety report |
| **Evidence Package** | White paper + stakeholder workshop (BLE, DINUM, EU AI Office) | Policy recommendations |

## Why Spain AgriFoodTech Sandbox

Spain is the **first comprehensive agrifood sandbox in the EU**. Our project produces exactly the "regulatory, normative, and procedural learnings" the Sandbox is designed for — but for **digital infrastructure**, not just food science. This positions Spain as the leader in EU agrifood regulatory innovation.
