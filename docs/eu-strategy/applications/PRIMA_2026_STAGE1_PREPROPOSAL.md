## 2. Excellence

### 2.1 Objectives

MED-TRACE will build a **context-aware digital traceability layer** for Mediterranean grain value chains that integrates:
1. **Real-time IoT + inspection data** from farm to port.
2. **EBSI-compliant Verifiable Credentials** for cross-border quality certification.
3. **Federated CEADS data-space participation** for policy monitoring.
4. **Voice-AI accessibility tools** for smallholder farmers with low digital literacy.

### 2.2 Concept & Innovation

Current traceability systems (e.g., TRACES NT for phytosanitary) are **government-centric and reactive**. MED-TRACE is **market-centric and proactive**: buyers verify quality before shipment, farmers prove provenance to access premium markets, and public authorities receive real-time food-security data via the Data Act's "exceptional need" API.

**Innovation leap:** Instead of tracing *problems* (recalls, fraud), we trace *trust* — cryptographic, automated, and cross-border from day one.
