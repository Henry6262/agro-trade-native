02 / 08

Two institutional bottlenecks

Settlement Friction

Cross-border commodity payments take 30–90 days. FX losses eat 3–7% of value. Wire transfers leave no audit trail for regulators. In Bulgaria, 40% of grain deals fail due to payment delays.

Compliance Darkness

KYC is a PDF scan. KYT does not exist for commodity flows. Travel Rule data is missing. AML thresholds are ignored. FINMA and MiCA require what the market cannot deliver.

The result: banks cannot touch agricultural trade. Farmers cannot get working capital. Buyers cannot verify supply chains.

04 / 08

Compliance by design — not bolted on

Built for regulated institutions from day one. Every transaction is auditable, traceable, and regulator-ready.

KYC

Privy tiered identity: Basic, Enhanced, Institutional. Google OAuth + JWT. Admin freeze capability.

KYT

Every escrow movement is a TradeEvent with on-chain hash, actor role, timestamp, and metadata.

AML

Jurisdiction-configurable thresholds. Suspicious pattern auto-flagging. Admin-gated escrow release.

Travel Rule

Full originator/beneficiary capture per Swiss & EU TFR. Compliant data available for VASP-to-VASP sharing.

FINMA Trade-conditional escrow structure = not a deposit. MiCA CASP-aligned custody model.

The RWA verification layer

Without verified quality data, escrow is just a locked box. SkyInspect is the key.

Autonomous Inspection

Drone with RTK-GNSS, NIR spectrometry, and edge AI. 45-second on-site quality scoring vs. 3-day lab wait.

EBSI Credentials

EU Blockchain Services Infrastructure verifiable quality credentials. Cross-border recognized from Bulgaria to Germany.

Oracle Feed

Inspection data feeds directly into escrow state. Underperformance triggers smart-contract pro-rata refunds. No court needed.

This turns physical grain into a verified, auditable on-chain asset — the missing data layer for RWA-backed commodity vaults.
