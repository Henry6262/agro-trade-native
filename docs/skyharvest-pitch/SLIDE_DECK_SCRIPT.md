# Slide Deck Script — SkyInspect + HarvestShares (Reframed)

> **Total duration: 3 minutes (180 seconds)**  
> **Slides: 8**  
> **Strategic frame:** China validated the technology. The EU mandated the data infrastructure. We are the only team building at the intersection.  
> **Geographic focus:** Dobrich, Bulgaria → Extremadura, Spain → Munich, Germany → Valencia, Spain

---

## SLIDE 1 — THE HOOK
**Duration:** 0:00–0:15 (15 seconds)  
**Visual:** Black screen → drone rotor sound → title card "SKYINSPECT"  
**On-screen text:** *"China flies 28,000 agricultural drones every day. What if Europe had the regulated version?"*

**Script:**
> "China flies twenty-eight thousand agricultural drones every day. What if Europe had the regulated version?"

**[PAUSE — 2 seconds]**

> "Three seconds. From touch-down to verdict."

**Delivery:** Slow. Let each word land. Make eye contact with one person.

**Stage direction:** Stand still. No gestures. The words are the weapon.

---

## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM
**Duration:** 0:15–0:45 (30 seconds)  
**Visual:** Split screen — inspector driving 4 hours across Balkans / Spanish olive farmer with empty pockets  
**On-screen text:** Two bottlenecks with numbers

**Script:**
> "Agricultural trade has two impossible bottlenecks — and Europe is still using phone calls and paper."

**[Gesture: left hand up]**

> "Inspection. A human drives four hours across the Balkans, takes a sample, waits three days for a lab in Sofia. One hundred and fifty euros. Fifteen percent of deals die waiting."

**[Gesture: right hand up]**

> "And farmer financing. A small farmer in Extremadura plants olives in spring. Gets paid ninety days after harvest. Cannot afford fertilizer without a predatory loan at twenty-five percent."

**[Bring hands together, then apart]**

> "While China flies twenty-eight thousand drones a day, a Bulgarian farmer near Dobrich still waits three days for a lab result. That gap is our opportunity."

**Delivery:** Building frustration. Not angry — resolved.

---

## SLIDE 3 — SKYINSPECT
**Duration:** 0:45–1:25 (40 seconds)  
**Visual:** 15-second drone sequence + split-screen dashboard with "EBSI CREDENTIAL" stamp  
**On-screen text:** "Inspector replaced by algorithm. 4 hours → 45 seconds. €300 → €12."

**Script:**
> "SkyInspect. The same DJI hardware China deploys at scale — optimized for EU regulatory requirements."

**[Point to screen as video plays]**

> "GPS waypoint navigation. RTK precision to two centimeters. Zhejiang University proved force-aware aerial manipulation. We're commercializing it for sixteen-hectare European farms, not point-six-hectare Chinese plots."

**[Pause for dashboard reveal]**

> "Sterile probe extracts fifty grams of tissue. On-device NIR spectrometry. Edge AI running Gemma Four. Quality score: zero to one hundred. Moisture. Protein. Contamination. EBSI-verifiable credential."

**[Turn back to audience]**

> "Four hours to forty-five seconds. Three hundred euros to twelve. The inspector is obsolete. And in the EU, that inspection data is now a legal requirement."

**Delivery:** Crescendo during tech specs. Punch the last line.

---

## SLIDE 4 — HARVESTSHARES
**Duration:** 1:25–1:55 (30 seconds)  
**Visual:** Winter-to-summer transition Dobrich + buyer app screen  
**On-screen text:** "Pinduoduo proved it for 800M consumers. We're building it for European institutional buyers."

**Script:**
> "HarvestShares. Pinduoduo proved fractional crop sales work for eight hundred million Chinese consumers. We're adding blockchain escrow and cross-border stablecoin settlement for European institutional buyers."

**[Gesture: planting a seed motion]**

> "Winter. A farmer near Dobrich lists batch twenty-eight forty-seven. One hundred hectares of winter wheat. A buyer in Munich buys thirty percent. A buyer in Valencia buys twenty. The farmer gets cUSD now — settled in three seconds."

**[Gesture: growing motion]**

> "Spring. Summer. The drone verifies. AI score: eighty-seven. EBSI credential issued. France-Germany cross-border recognized."

**[Gesture: harvest motion]**

> "Harvest. Grain delivered to the Danube port. Escrow releases. Buyers receive their share. If the crop fails — drought, flood, disease — smart contract pro-rata refund. No court. No broker. No ninety-day wait."

**[Pause]**

> "The farmer in Dobrich doesn't need a bank. He needs a buyer in Stuttgart who believes his wheat will grow. We make that belief enforceable, cross-border, and regulator-auditable."

**Delivery:** Warm. Human. This is the emotional core.

**Duration:** 1:55–2:25 (30 seconds)  
**Visual:** 9-phase horizontal timeline animation (add EBSI credential icon)  
**On-screen text:** Icons only

**[Point at each icon as you speak]**

> "China proved the drone. Pinduoduo proved the marketplace. We built the compliance layer that makes both legal in the European Union."

**Delivery:** Rhythmic. Almost military precision. One word per icon.

## SLIDE 6 — WHY NOW
**Duration:** 2:25–2:40 (15 seconds)  
**Visual:** Three converging forces table  
**On-screen text:** China proved it. EU mandated it. We're the only team building both.

**Script:**
> "Three forces converging. China proved the technology at scale — twenty-eight thousand flights a day, eighty-nine to ninety-four percent AI accuracy. The EU wrote the regulation — Data Act, CEADS, EBSI. And we are the only team building at the intersection of both."

**[Pause]**

> "Chinese players are locked out by regulation. Western players don't have the agrifood plus blockchain plus compliance integration. We own this quadrant."

**Delivery:** Confident. Almost casual. This is inevitability.

---

## SLIDE 7 — THE ASK
**Duration:** 2:40–2:55 (15 seconds)  
**Visual:** Four clean icons with numbers + map showing Dobrich and Valencia  
**On-screen text:** €100K + 2 partnerships = EU-wide validated infrastructure

**Script:**
> "We are not asking for belief. We are asking for fuel."

**[Count on fingers]**

> "Sixty thousand for three drones and EU operator certification. Forty thousand for smart contract audit and legal framework. One pilot farm in Dobrich. One committed buyer."

**[Pause]**

> "Bulgaria gives us the grain corridor. Spain gives us the AgriFoodTech sandbox and olive diversity. One hundred thousand euros. Two handshakes. Six months to validated autonomous agrifood infrastructure for the Balkans and Iberian Peninsula."

**Delivery:** Direct. No filler. Each number is a nail.

**Duration:** 2:55–3:00 (5 seconds)  
**Visual:** Drone flying into sunset over Dobrich + QR code + map corridor  
**On-screen text:** "AgroTrade. SkyInspect. HarvestShares."

**Script:**

**[Pause]**

> "The future of European food is not a guess. It is programmable."

**[Smile. Stop. Breathe.]**

> "Thank you. The drone is ready. The contracts are written. We just need the runway."

**Delivery:** Quiet. Let the line echo. Then stop.

## PRACTICE NOTES

- Record yourself. Watch for filler words ("um", "like", "so").
- The pause after "Three seconds" is sacred. Do not rush it.
- Do not read from notes. Internalize the structure, not the exact words.
- If you finish early, extend the pauses. Silence is power.
- If you run long, cut from Slide 5 (Integrated Flow) first — the timeline is visual, words are secondary.
- **Practice saying "Dobrich" and "Extremadura" correctly.** Mispronouncing your pilot locations kills credibility.
